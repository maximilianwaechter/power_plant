U.S Electric Power Generation

Biomass, Geothermal, and Solar Power Plants in the U.S.

Map authored by Max Waechter.

Design principle applied: visual hierarchy and clarity.

I increased the size contrast between small and large plants and used different symbol shapes (circles for biomass, squares for geothermal, and triangles for solar) so the most important plants and categories stand out more clearly.

This makes it easier for users to quickly see which plants have higher capacity and to distinguish energy types at a glance, improving the overall reading of the map.
